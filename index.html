How To - Ideas
mindFULLness
"This is something I should know how to do before I leave MICA."

The learning curve has been steep and mostly satisfying. I have a lot more to learn before I feel comfortable with illustrator, photoshop, or CSS. What I am walking away is a solid footing in the right tools. None of this dumbed down user-friendly tomfoolery! (Piknic, wix, iphoto)

More valuable than those technical skills was the opportunity to look through the lens of design and find insight into things I found to be interesting, unjust or beautiful. Trying to communicate something in the vocabulary of design means unpacking it and really looking at it up close. You have to have a clear idea in your head about how and why something is important before you start to design something around it.
