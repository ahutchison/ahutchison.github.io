Go with the Flow - No Strings Attached

For my project I chose to create a brand that opposed the DivaCup; a menstrual cup marketed as an alternative to disposable pads and tampons. I think they have a wonderful product but do a terrible job marketing it to the average woman.

Because menstrual cups are reusable they have a significantly smaller environmental impact as compared to pads and tampons. They are a one time investment, completely sanitary and more reliable (mess-wise) than the alternative. The feminine hygene industry creates wasteful products that foster a culture of embarassment around the functions of women's bodies. It's about hiding. My product will be different.

My goal in marketing Go With the Flow and No Strings Attached to to make a visible product that becomes as mainstream as pads and tampons.
